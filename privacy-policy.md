# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Final Note ("the App"), developed by Qi Su, handles your information.

---

## 1. Data Storage

Final Note stores all data **locally on your device only**. This includes:

- Vault entries (account names, usernames, passwords, notes)
- Letters you write
- Personal settings (date of birth, lifespan preference)

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.** Your data never leaves your device.

---

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Device identifiers
- Any content you enter into the App

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Final Note does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

---

## 4. Face ID / Touch ID

The App uses Apple's Face ID and Touch ID frameworks solely to authenticate access to protected entries. Biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's secure enclave on your device. We never access or store biometric data.

---

## 5. iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your App data may be included in your device backup managed by Apple. This is controlled entirely by your iOS settings and Apple's privacy policy. We have no access to your iCloud data.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Final Note is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
